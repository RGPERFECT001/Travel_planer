Attractions in Ladakh

Nature Hills

Pangong Tso

Pangong Tso is a beautiful endorheic lake situated in the Himalayas and is 134 km long, extending from India to China. The lake is situated at a height of 4,350 m and is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Ladakh.

Nature Lake

Hunder Sand Dunes

The Hunder Sand Dunes are a cold desert located in Nubra Valley, Ladakh. The sand dunes are a unique attraction in Ladakh and are a must-visit for tourists. The sand dunes are surrounded by snow-capped mountains and are a beautiful sight to behold.

Historical Memorial

Dras War Memorial

The Dras War Memorial is a war memorial built by the Indian Army in memory of the soldiers who lost their lives in the Kargil War. The memorial is located in the foothills of the Tololing Hill in Dras, Kargil District, Jammu and Kashmir.

Wandoor beach is one of the most relaxing and rejuvenating places in Port Blair. The beach is full of biodiversity and the water is crystal clear.

Attractions in Goa

Beach Party

Baga Beach

Baga Beach is one of the most popular beaches in Goa and is known for its nightlife and water sports activities. The beach is lined with shacks and restaurants that serve delicious Goan cuisine.

Beach Party

Calangute Beach

Calangute Beach is one of the most popular beaches in Goa and is known for its water sports activities and nightlife. The beach is lined with shacks and restaurants that serve delicious Goan cuisine.

Historical Monument

Basilica of Bom Jesus

The Basilica of Bom Jesus is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is one of the oldest churches in Goa. The church is known for housing the mortal remains of St. Francis Xavier.

Attractions in Dehradun

Robber's Cave

Robber's Cave is a natural cave formation located near Dehradun. The cave is known for its natural beauty and is a popular tourist attraction in Dehradun.

Lacchiwala

Lacchiwala is a popular picnic spot located near Dehradun. The spot is known for its natural beauty and is a great place to relax and unwind.

Forest Research Institute

The Forest Research Institute is a premier institution for forestry research in India. The institute is known for its beautiful architecture and is a popular tourist attraction in Dehradun.
